About Us

Established in 2018 by two board game enthusiasts, Boardgame Bazaar has gone from strength to strength. What was just an idea born from an exuberant gaming session in the Dice Box gaming café in Agricola has grown into one of the fastest growing businesses in Brasshire.

We stock all sorts of games for the enthusiast, from niche complex games to family-friendly games to introduce the whole family to this great hobby.

You can be sure of a great shopping experience with Boardgame Bazaar and we are certain with great prices we will become your number one stop for latest and greatest in table top entertainment. Be sure to check back often as we expand our customer experience

UK shipping costs start and end at £4.00 per order, regardless of how many items you have ordered.

If you are in the area, you can always visit our warehouse and chat with the team; you never know, we may have time for a quick game!

We accept payments by credit or debit cards.
